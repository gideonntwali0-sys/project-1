Seasonal Gardening Tips

Expert advice to keep your garden thriving throughout the year

Spring Awakening

March - May

Spring is the perfect time to prepare your garden for the growing season ahead.

  • Prune dead branches and shape shrubs
  • Apply fresh mulch to suppress weeds
  • Start seeds indoors for summer vegetables
  • Divide perennials before new growth begins
  • Test and amend soil as needed

Summer Splendor

June - August

Keep your garden lush and vibrant during the hottest months of the year.

  • Water deeply in early morning or evening
  • Deadhead flowers to encourage blooming
  • Monitor for pests and treat organically
  • Harvest vegetables regularly for best flavor
  • Add fresh annuals for continuous color

Autumn Preparation

September - November

Prepare your garden for winter while enjoying fall's beautiful transformation.

  • Plant spring-blooming bulbs now
  • Collect and compost fallen leaves
  • Protect tender plants from frost
  • Clean and store garden tools properly
  • Plan next year's garden layout
